<td style="font-family:Arial;font-weight:bold;text-align:left;font-size:10pt;margin-bottom:0pt;margin-top:0pt;">Election of New Director.</td> </tr> </table> <p style="font-family:Arial;text-align:left;font-size:10pt;margin-bottom:0px;margin-top:6pt">On November&#160;13, 2019, the Board of Directors (&#8220;Board&#8221;) of Intel Corporation (&#8220;Intel&#8221;) elected Mr.&#160;James (Jim) J. Goetz to the Board, effective immediately. The Board determined that Mr.&#160;Goetz qualifies as &#8220;independent&#8221; in accordance with the published listing requirements of Nasdaq. Mr.&#160;Goetz has not been appointed to any Board committees at this time. </p> <p style="font-family:Arial;text-align:left;font-size:10pt;margin-bottom:0px;margin-top:12pt">Mr.&#160;Goetz, age 54, has served as a partner of Sequoia Capital Operations, LLC, a venture capital firm, since June&#160;2004. Prior to joining Sequoia, Mr.&#160;Goetz <span style="white-space:nowrap">co-founded</span> VitalSigns Software, where he assembled and led the team that pioneered <span style="white-space:nowrap">end-user</span> performance management. Prior to VitalSigns, he was the Vice President of Network Management for Bay Networks. Mr.&#160;Goetz currently serves on the boards of several privately held companies, and since April 2005, on the board of Palo Alto Networks, Inc., a network security solution company. He previously served on the boards of directors of Barracuda Networks, Inc., a data security and storage company from 2009 to 2017; Nimble Storage, Inc., a data storage company, from 2007 to 2017; Jive Software, Inc., a provider of social business software, from 2007 to 2015; and Ruckus Wireless, Inc., a manufacturer of wireless<span style="white-space:nowrap">&#160;(Wi-Fi)</span>&#160;networking equipment, from 2012 to 2015.
